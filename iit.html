IIT

missions and visions

Founded in 2012, IIT offers an innovative educational model in partnership with national and international universities and schools, implementing a semester-based structure and programs inspired by the North American system.

IIT is committed to creating an environment that promotes skill development and innovation among its students.We strive to offer a complete range of high-quality services to support our students’ projects.we aim to increase external funding for scientific research activities.We aspire to contribute to building a national ecosystem, open internationally, based on an entrepreneurial economy.We adopt academic programs that evolve with new methodologies and in cooperation with prestigious international universities.Our goal is to train a generation of individuals equipped with personal abilities, cultural knowledge, communication skills, as well as scientific and professional competencies.

Our vision is to orient the academic process toward meeting the needs of society’s development.We aim for excellence in teaching methods, training, integration, and scientific research.We plan to play an active role in improving higher education and scientific research by working closely with our partners at both the national and international levels.We intend to adopt new scientific disciplines that align with scientific and technological advancements and address society’s needs.We aspire to establish models of cooperation between the International Institute of Technology and other components of society, whether economic, social, or cultural.

accreditations

The International Institute of Technology of Sfax (IIT) is recognized nationally and internationally for its excellence and rigor. Its mission is to equip graduates with the skills and knowledge needed for a globalized professional world. IIT has earned the EUR-ACE® and EURO-INF® international accreditations, ensuring its programs meet high international standards. These achievements make IIT a leader in higher education in southern Tunisia and enhance its global visibility.
