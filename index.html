Hi,It's Aram

I'm a Computer Engineering student at IIT Sfax. Before joining IIT, I spent two years in the Math–Physics preparatory classes in Sousse and passed the national exam. My ranking placed me in Electromechanical Engineering — and I still keep calling it “electromagnetic engineering” by mistake because I struggled so much with physics that I basically invented a new major . Even though physics opened my eyes and changed the way I see the world, I realized that my real interest was somewhere else. I chose Computer Science because it touches so many fields and gives me the freedom to build any future I want. I want to work in areas that actually matter and create impact.If I only followed my hobbies, I would probably be studying game design or filmmaking — I’d love to be a movie director someday. But I see those as passions. A career, for me, is something that makes a difference. And I’m sure that once I find my place, I’ll end up loving what I do.
